Tie it Off and Count Again
April 2nd - 7th, 2018

Tie it Off and Count Again, was hosted by the Hamilton Audio Visual Node in Hamilton, Ontario, from April 2nd to 7th, 2018. The exhibition represented textile work by women and non-binary artists including:

Vida Beyer
Cat Blumke
Sophia Borowska
Adrienne Hall
Jaichen Liu
Maddy McNeely
Soledad Fatima Muñoz
Maria Jose Murillo
